Formula SAE is an international competition where student teams design, fabricate, and race cars to a set of rules called the formula. Bulldogs Racing is Yale University's chapter of Formula SAE, which has competed in some form of the competition since 2007. From 2015-2016 I was Chief Engineer of the team, during which time I lead design and fabrication of the suspension, steering, brakes, and chassis portions of the car.
